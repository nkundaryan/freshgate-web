Who We Are

Rooted in Rwanda.
Built for the World.

Freshgate East Africa Ltd is a Rwandan agribusiness dedicated to growing premium export-quality produce. From hillside avocado orchards to state-of-the-art greenhouse operations, we combine traditional knowledge of Rwanda's fertile land with modern agricultural techniques.

Our mission is to connect the abundant potential of East African agriculture to buyers and partners across the globe — with full traceability, consistent quality, and a commitment to the communities we grow in.

◈
Quality First Every batch is graded to export standards — clean, sized, and traceable from farm to freight.
◈
Sustainable by Design Our greenhouses and orchard management practices are built for long-term soil health and water efficiency.
◈
Partnership Minded We work closely with buyers, investors, and local cooperatives to build lasting, mutually beneficial relationships.

What sets us apart

01

Traceable from Farm to Freight

We maintain full lot traceability from planting through harvest and packing — giving buyers the documentation and confidence they need.

02

Consistent, Reliable Supply

Our combination of orchard crops and greenhouse production means we can offer year-round supply with predictable volumes and quality.

Investor & Partner Ready

Community Rooted

Freshgate was built by Rwandans, for Rwanda. We are invested in the local economy and in reducing the country's dependence on imported produce.
